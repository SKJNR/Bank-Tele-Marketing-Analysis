Tele Marketing Analysis

Portuguese bank in order to design strategies for improving future marketing campaigns. The object of this campaign is to pursue customers to subscribe the term deposit. The Marketing campaign was based on phone calls. The classification goal is to predict if the client will subscribe a term deposit (variable y).
